Intro

Hi, my name is Danish (not like the pastry), every person has a unique journey that shapes who they are and who they will become -- here is a snippet of mine.

Throughout my younger life, I often found myself rushing to make decisions, which ultimately left me feeling indecisive about my career path. However, I believe that these experiences have played a crucial role in shaping my perspective and guiding me towards my true passion.

To be honest, I never considered a career in software engineering until recently. It was the world of modding communities for PC games that truly captured my attention and ignited a deep fascination within me. Witnessing the incredible creations and the creative power of individuals in those communities, I couldn't help but feel a longing to acquire the skills necessary to bring my own ideas to life, not just in the form of mods.

While I may have made what some would consider "wrong choices" in the past, I firmly believe that those choices have contributed to my personal growth and have led me to this point. They have taught me valuable lessons, instilling in me a sense of adaptability and resilience. I now understand that every decision, regardless of the outcome, plays a part in shaping our journey.

I have embraced the world of software engineering with open arms, recognizing that it offers a perfect blend of creativity and technical expertise, something which I long for. Through my experiences, I have developed a unique perspective that fuels my drive to create innovative and enjoyable experience for users.

I am eager to continue my journey, honing my skills and pushing boundaries in the realm of software engineering. With a solid foundation of life lessons and a newfound passion, I am excited to explore the possibilities that lie ahead. I am confident that my unconventional path and diverse experiences have prepared me to contribute my unique insights to the world of software engineering.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a little about me. I am excited to embark on this exciting journey, and I look forward to the challenges and opportunities that await.

By the way, check out my projects.

Projects

Chillify

Python | JavaScript | Flask | PostgreSQL | React | Redux | AWS S3 | HTML5 | CSS3

Chillify is a Spotify soft-clone. Users are able to log-in/register, view other user's public playlists, view genres, create playlists, add music to playlists, like music, and of course, listen to music.

Phillyroll

Python | JavaScript | Flask | PostgreSQL | React | Redux | AWS S3 | HTML5 | CSS3

Phillyroll is a collaborative project made by myself and three other developers. This project is inspired by Crunchyroll, an anime streaming platform. Users can view shows and watch episodes belonging to the show.

AirLnL

JavaScript | Express.js | PostgreSQL | React | Redux | AWS S3 | HTML5 | CSS3

AirLnL is a project inspired by AirBnB. User's can currently log-in/register, view posted spots, and submit reviews for spots belonging to other users.
